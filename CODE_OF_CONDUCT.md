# Code of Conduct

Nivotics Lab is committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, nationality, or identity.

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by Nivotics Lab, including GitHub repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and any community channels.

---

## Our Standards

Behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Welcoming contributors of all experience levels
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback graciously
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community
- Showing patience, especially with learners and first-time contributors

Behaviour that is not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissive or condescending language toward any contributor
- Publishing private information about others without consent
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative setting

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the Nivotics Lab team at nivoticslab@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and to temporarily or permanently exclude contributors who engage in harmful behaviour.

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
